FAQ

What is digital piracy?

Digital piracy is the unauthorized reproduction or distribution of copyrighted digital content without the consent of the rights holder. It encompasses activities like downloading, sharing, or distributing copyrighted material without proper authorization.

Is digital piracy illegal?

Yes it is.

Are there any arguments in favor of digital piracy?

Some argue that digital piracy can lead to increased exposure for creators and their work, potentially benefiting them in the long run. However, this perspective is a subject of ongoing debate.

Is streaming copyrighted content without permission considered digital piracy?

Yes, streaming copyrighted content without proper authorization is considered a form of digital piracy, as it involves the unauthorized distribution of copyrighted material.

Where can I find legal alternatives for accessing digital content?

Legal alternatives for accessing digital content include licensed streaming services, official online stores, and platforms that offer content through authorized channels. These services ensure that creators are compensated for their work.
