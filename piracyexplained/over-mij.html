Why have I chosen this topic?

I have chosen this topic because I have always been interested in the works behind piracy. I was also interested in how "internet-pirates" stay anonymous, I quickly figured out this was doing by using VPN's (Virtual Private Networks) to hide their IP address from the public. Also a thing to point out is that torrenting is also very useful outside of the piracy space, it's a great way to share files peer to peer between users since they would not have to rely on a service or a provider, they would be the provider itself in that case a great example of that would be Linux distros being uploaded and kept up on torrent. I also believe that it is important for people to realise what potential conquences there are behind digital piracy.
